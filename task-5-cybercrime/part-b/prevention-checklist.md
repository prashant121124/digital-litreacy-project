-Prevention Tips
1. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
3. Do not approve unknown payment requests
4. Always verify the sender before making payments
5. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
6. Use strong passwords and enable 2-factor authentication
7. Regularly check your bank statements and transactions
8. Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store
9. Log out from shared or public devices


-UPI-Specific Safety
1. Remember: You receive money without entering a PIN
2. Always double-check the name before sending money


-Reporting Channels (IMPORTANT)
1. Report fraud at: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
2. Call Helpline: 1930 📞
