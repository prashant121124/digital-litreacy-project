-Prevention Tips
Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
Do not approve unknown payment requests
Always verify the sender before making payments
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
Use strong passwords and enable 2-factor authentication
Regularly check your bank statements and transactions
Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store
Log out from shared or public devices


-UPI-Specific Safety
Remember: You receive money without entering a PIN
Always double-check the name before sending money


-Reporting Channels (IMPORTANT)
Report fraud at: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
Call Helpline: 1930 📞
