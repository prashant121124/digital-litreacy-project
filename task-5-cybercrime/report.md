While researching UPI fraud, I was surprised by how easily scammers can manipulate people using simple tricks like fake calls and payment requests. I realized that many victims fall into the trap due to lack of awareness and urgency created by fraudsters.

One important thing I learned is that no legitimate service ever asks for sensitive details like OTPs or UPI PINs. Even a small mistake, such as approving an unknown request, can lead to financial loss.

As a result of this task, I will be more cautious while using digital payment apps. I will always verify requests before taking any action and avoid sharing personal or financial information with anyone.
