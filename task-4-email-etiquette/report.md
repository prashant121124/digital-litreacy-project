In this task, I learned how to write professional emails and follow proper email etiquette. I drafted two emails: one to a professor requesting an assignment extension and another to an internship coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship. While writing these emails, I ensured that each email included a clear subject line, a formal greeting, a well-structured body, and a polite closing.

I understood that professional communication requires clarity, respect, and proper formatting. Using formal language and maintaining a polite tone helps create a positive impression on the recipient. I also learned the importance of addressing the recipient appropriately, such as using “Dear Professor [Last Name]” or “Dear Hiring Manager.”

This task helped me realize that effective email communication is an essential skill for academic and professional success.
